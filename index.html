Tate Criqui

Tate Criqui is a freshman computer science major from San Diego, CA.

Previously, he has worked with The League of Amazing Programmers (a nonprofit organization for young coders), teaching classes on Java and Python programming.

He has also created several large-scale projects, such as an action-adventure game, a Discord bot, and a motion detection system using computer vision. For his high school capstone engineering project, he collaborated with NexStream Technical Education to create a curriculum for a digital electronics college class.

Outside of his work and studies, Tate also enjoys hiking, reading, and acting.
In high school, he featured in several plays, and was at different times a starring role, stage manager/lighting technician, and assistant director. He also wrote and staged an original whodunnit comedic play.
He additionally took up a leadership role in several clubs, guiding fellow students through improv comedy, classical guitar, and dramatic speech.
Plus, he's fun to be around.
