Tate Criqui's Personal Website

Tate Criqui is a freshman computer science major from San Diego, CA.

Previously, he has worked with The League of Amazing Programmers (a nonprofit organization for young coders), teaching classes on Java and Python programming.
He has also created several large-scale projects, such as a small action-adventure game, a Discord bot,
and a motion detection system using computer vision.

Outside of work and school, he also enjoys hiking, reading, and acting.
In high school, he featured in several plays, and was at different times a starring role, stage manager/lighting technician, and assistant director. He also wrote and staged an original whodunnit comedic play.
Plus, he's fun to be around.
